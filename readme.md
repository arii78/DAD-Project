PROJECT BACKGROUND(TAILOR MANAGEMENT SYSTEM)

-A system to records details on the customer who ordered many types of clothes. 
-The system consists of customer’s information and details on the order including measurement of the clothes. 
-The user for the system are staff and customers who make order. 
-The purpose of the system is to avoid the user from wasting time and energy as well as wasting paper.
-Can save more space and avoid the document store from scattering.
-Manager can keep all data about his customer instead of keeping the records in file.
